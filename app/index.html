Tips For CHOOSING a Broker

Trade Commissions

You are completely free to buy and sell shares, bonds and other investments. This is usually cheap, which is an advantage. A disadvantage is that you have to do everything yourself and have some knowledge of stock exchange.

Customer Service

You do not buy separate shares but you participate in funds: baskets of shares within a theme. An advantage is that you invest in such a spread, almost effortlessly. The extra fund management costs are a disadvantage.

Trading Tools

You let someone else do the work, but you remain responsible. The investing is automated based on a profile. Usually you pay a percentage of the total investment value as compensation. Letting investing is accessible to everyone.

Account Minimums

You leave the investing entirely to a specialist. There is a strong entry threshold and the compensation is high. With real serious capabilities and a fixed point of contact, we call this private banking .
