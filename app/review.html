Full Review

Merrill Edge appeals to casual traders with a powerful combination of strong customer service and competitive pricing. The online broker integrates seamlessly with parent company Bank of America: Customers can access accounts at both with a single login, and Merrill Edge brokerage clients have access to investment advice at more than 2,100 Bank of America locations. Those who meet minimum balance requirements qualify for a limited number of commission-free trades each month.

Merrill Edge is best for:

  • Investors who value customer service
  • Bank of America customers
  • High-balance customers
  • Penny stock traders
